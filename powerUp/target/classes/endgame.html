End of the game

There are three ways in which the game can end: a company reaching size 50, two companies reaching size 30, and any player running out of tiles when there are no more remaining to be drawn.

Procedure

Every active company at the end of the game is sold and liquidated, exactly as if it were the subject of a takeover during the game. More information on the precise procedure is available on the "Mergers" tab.

Rankings

After the game has ended, each player will hold only cash, and no shares. Whichever player has the most cash is the winner, and every other player is ranked in descending order of wealth.
